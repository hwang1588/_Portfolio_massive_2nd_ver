Massive protocol

Massive protocol is a service for ‘Creators’ who form and contribute Fan groups through Creator Economics.
Massive protocol covers a variety of areas, including financial platforms for creators (social token issuance and trading), NFT Marketplace, Influencer, and Advertising Partnerships.

Creator Token
Creator's Ecosystem

Every creator, artist, musician, gamer, celebrity, athlete, or content producer builds their own token economy around fan tokens and NFTs.
Creator tokens are used for community loyalty, rewards for participation, and create special benefits or rewards for holders (fans).
