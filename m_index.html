01

For Creator

Massive protocol

Massive protocol is a service for ‘Creators’ who form and contribute Fan groups through Creator Economics. Massive protocol covers a variety of areas, including financial platforms for creators (social token issuance and trading), NFT Marketplace, Influencer, and Advertising Partnerships.

02

Creator Token

Creator’s Ecosystem

Every creator, artist, musician, gamer, celebrity, athlete, or content producer builds their own token economy around fan tokens and NFTs. Creator tokens are used for community loyalty, rewards for participation, and create special benefits or rewards for holders (fans).

03

Community

FAN

Using and holding the creator's coins who they support, they show loyalty and support for the creator, and the creator coin owner can receive pre-sale access, exclusive products and discounts, and receive $MAV compensation depending on the community activity. Also, depositing $MAV tokens, can enjoy a variety of services, including staking income and offering a discounts and promotions.
